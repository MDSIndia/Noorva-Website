## Overview

Noorva is a next-generation Human-Interactive AI Companion designed to help people live smarter, more productive, and more fulfilling lives.

Unlike traditional AI assistants that only respond to commands, Noorva acts as a proactive companion, mentor, guide, and advisor that continuously learns from users and evolves alongside them.

Noorva combines:

* Human Interactive AI
* Noorva Guide
* Mood Based Intelligence
* Advanced Source Filtering
* Smart Reminders
* Voice Companion
* Personalized Guidance

to create a truly human-centered AI experience.
